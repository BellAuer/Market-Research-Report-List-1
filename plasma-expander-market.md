<p><p>The plasma expander market encompasses various products used to increase intravascular volume during medical treatments. Dextran and Hydroxyethyl Starch are synthetic polymers designed to enhance fluid retention in blood vessels. Human Albumin, derived from human plasma, provides essential proteins for volume expansion. PEGylated Albumin offers extended circulation time due to polyethylene glycol modification. Polyvinylpyrrolidone (PVP) serves as a synthetic plasma expander with low side effects, while Gelatin, derived from animal sources, provides a biocompatible alternative for volume restoration.</p></p>
