<p><ul><li>Analog TMR Sensing ICs</li><li>Digital TMR Sensing ICs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TMR (Tunnel Magnetoresistance) Sensing ICs are categorized into two main types: Analog and Digital. Analog TMR Sensing ICs provide a continuous output that varies with the magnetic field strength, making them suitable for applications requiring precise measurements, such as in industrial sensors and motor control. In contrast, Digital TMR Sensing ICs deliver discrete output signals, often designed for simple on/off or position detection applications, which enhances compatibility with digital systems and simplifies integration into electronic devices.</p></p>
