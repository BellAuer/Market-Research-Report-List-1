<p><strong>The Extrusion Coating Grade LDPE Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tubular Process</li><li>Autoclave Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Extrusion Coating Grade LDPE market primarily consists of two types: the Tubular Process and the Autoclave Process. The Tubular Process involves extruding molten LDPE through a circular die to create a tube, which is then flattened to form a film. This method is beneficial for high-volume production and efficient film thickness control. The Autoclave Process, on the other hand, utilizes pressure vessels to polymerize LDPE, allowing for enhanced control over molecular weight distribution and improved film properties, making it suitable for specialized applications.</p></p>
