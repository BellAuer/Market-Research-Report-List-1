<p><ul><li>Optical System</li><li>Electromagnetic System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cranial navigation system market comprises two primary types: optical systems and electromagnetic systems. Optical systems utilize camera-based technology to track instruments and patient anatomy, providing precise spatial orientation for surgical procedures. In contrast, electromagnetic systems rely on magnetic fields to track the position of surgical tools, offering real-time feedback and flexibility in varied clinical settings. Both systems enhance surgical accuracy and patient safety, playing crucial roles in neurosurgery and other cranial procedures by improving navigation capabilities.</p></p>
