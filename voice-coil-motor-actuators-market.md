<p><p>Voice coil motor actuators are electromagnetic devices used for precise motion control. The Moving Coil Type features a coil attached to a movable element placed within a magnetic field. When current passes through the coil, it generates a force that creates linear motion. In contrast, the Moving Magnet Type has a fixed coil with a magnet on the moving element. This design allows for higher efficiency and better performance in applications requiring compact size and fast response, such as robotics and optical systems.</p></p>
