<p><p>Data Center Busway refers to a modular power distribution system that delivers electrical power efficiently and safely within data centers. This system comprises a series of conductive paths or busbars enclosed in protective casings, facilitating the easy distribution of electricity from the main source to multiple equipment loads. <p><strong>The Data Center Busway Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-Phase 4-Wire</li><li>3-Phase 5-Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Center Busway Market is characterized by two primary types: 3-Phase 4-Wire and 3-Phase 5-Wire systems. The 3-Phase 4-Wire configuration consists of three phase conductors and one neutral wire, commonly used for single-phase loads. In contrast, the 3-Phase 5-Wire system includes three phase conductors and two neutral wires, enabling better load balancing and higher capacity, making it suitable for more demanding applications. Both types are crucial for efficient power distribution in data centers.</p></p>
