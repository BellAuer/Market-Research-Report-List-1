<p><p>The espresso coffee machine market can be categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully-automatic machines. Manual machines require hands-on control for brewing, appealing to purists who value the art of coffee-making. Semi-automatic machines automate the water flow but allow users to manage the grinding and tamping, offering a balance of convenience and control. Fully-automatic machines handle all processes, including grinding and brewing, making them ideal for those seeking ease and speed in their coffee experience.</p></p>
