<p><p>Liquid rotameters, also known as variable area flowmeters, are devices used to measure the flow rate of liquids and gases. They operate on the principle of buoyancy, where a float rises or falls in a conical tube based on the flow rate of the fluid. As the flow increases, the float moves upward, allowing for easy visual measurement of liquid flow.</p><p>The liquid rotameters market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for precise flow measurement across various industries, including chemical processing, water treatment, and pharmaceuticals.
