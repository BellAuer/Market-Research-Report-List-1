<p><p>The cannabis extraction market comprises various methods, each serving distinct purposes. Sub/Supercritical CO2 extraction utilizes carbon dioxide under high pressure to isolate cannabinoids while preserving purity. Ethanol extraction employs food-grade ethanol, offering efficiency with lower production costs. Hydrocarbon extraction relies on solvents like butane or propane, known for high potency but requiring careful handling. Solvent-less extraction, including techniques like rosin pressing, avoids chemicals entirely, delivering a clean product. Other methods might include various novel and emerging technologies not defined by traditional categories.</p></p>
