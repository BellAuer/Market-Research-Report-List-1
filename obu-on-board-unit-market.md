<p><strong>The OBU (On Board Unit) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sngle-chip OBU (Without IC Card Interface)</li><li>Double-chip OBU (With IC Card Interface)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The On Board Unit (OBU) market consists of two main types: single-chip and double-chip OBUs. Single-chip OBUs operate without an integrated circuit (IC) card interface, providing a streamlined solution for basic vehicle tracking and toll collection. In contrast, double-chip OBUs include an IC card interface, allowing for additional functionalities like secure transactions and user identification. This differentiation caters to varying customer needs, with single-chip OBUs appealing to cost-sensitive users, while double-chip OBUs target those requiring advanced features.</p></p>
