One-component coatings are ready-to-use solutions that require no mixing, offering ease of application and reducing labor time. In contrast, two-component systems consist of a base and a hardener that must be mixed before application, providing enhanced performance and durability. Both types play a critical role in shielding structures from fire hazards, but their formulations and application processes differ significantly.</p></p>
