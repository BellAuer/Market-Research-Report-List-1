<p><ul><li>Capacitance Sensors</li><li>Eddy Current Sensors</li><li>Photoelectric Sensors</li><li>Ultrasonic Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear sensor market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. Capacitance sensors detect changes in electric field due to proximity of targets, ideal for non-contact detection. Eddy current sensors use electromagnetic fields to sense conductive materials, offering high precision in metal detection. Photoelectric sensors utilize light transmission to detect objects, suitable for presence detection in automation. Ultrasonic sensors employ sound waves for distance measurement, excelling in non-contact scenarios. "Others" encompass specialized sensors like laser and magnetic sensors.</p></p>
