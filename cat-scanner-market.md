<p><p>The CAT scanner market primarily includes two types: C-Arms and O-Arms. C-Arms are specialized imaging devices used in surgical settings, providing real-time X-ray visualization for various procedures. They are essential for minimally invasive surgeries. O-Arms, in contrast, are designed specifically for intraoperative imaging, offering 3D visualization capabilities during spinal surgeries and other complex procedures. Both technologies enhance surgical precision and patient outcomes, serving distinct yet complementary roles in modern medical imaging.</p></p>
