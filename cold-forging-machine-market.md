<p><p>Cold forging machines are categorized by the number of die stations they possess, impacting production capacity and complexity. The 2-die station is suitable for simple shapes, while the 3-die station offers enhanced versatility. The 4-die station allows for more intricate designs, and the 5-die station improves efficiency in higher volume production. The 6-die station further enhances capabilities, accommodating complex geometries. Other categories include 1-die and 7-die stations, catering to specialized applications and large-scale manufacturing needs, respectively.</p></p>
