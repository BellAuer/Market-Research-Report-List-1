<p><p>The automotive off-road vehicle lighting market comprises three key types: LED lighting, halogen lighting, and HID lighting. LED lighting is energy-efficient, durable, and offers bright illumination with lower power consumption, making it increasingly popular among off-road enthusiasts. Halogen lighting, while less efficient, provides good brightness and is cost-effective, though it generates more heat. HID lighting delivers intense brightness and improved visibility over longer distances, ideal for challenging terrains, but often comes with higher upfront costs and requires proper installation.</p></p>
