<p><p>The fully bioresorbable stents market can be segmented based on their strut thickness into three categories: below 100μm, 100-150μm, and more than 150μm. Stents below 100μm are designed for thinner profiles that promote better vessel healing and reduced thrombus formation. The 100-150μm range offers a balance between flexibility and strength. Stents exceeding 150μm provide enhanced durability and support but may have slower resorption rates. Each category addresses specific clinical needs and patient demographics, influencing adoption rates in different healthcare settings.</p></p>
