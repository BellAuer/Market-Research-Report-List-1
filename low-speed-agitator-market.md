<p><strong>The Low Speed Agitator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anchor Impeller</li><li>Helical Impeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low-speed agitator market includes types like anchor impellers and helical impellers, each designed for specific applications in mixing and blending. Anchor impellers feature a flat, wide surface that provides effective mixing in high-viscosity fluids by minimizing fluid slip along the container walls. Helical impellers, characterized by their twisted blades, enhance flow and are ideal for maintaining uniformity in moderate viscosity fluids. Both types are essential in industries like pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemicals for achieving desired consistency and homogeneity.</p></p>
