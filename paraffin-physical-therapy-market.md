<p><p>The paraffin physical therapy market is divided into two main types: ready-to-use paraffin and ready-to-heat paraffin. Ready-to-use paraffin comes pre-melted and is convenient for immediate application, typically used in clinics or at home for therapeutic purposes such as pain relief and improved joint mobility. In contrast, ready-to-heat paraffin requires the user to melt the wax before use, allowing for customization of temperature and consistency, making it suitable for controlled therapeutic sessions. Both types cater to different user preferences and therapeutic needs.</p></p>
