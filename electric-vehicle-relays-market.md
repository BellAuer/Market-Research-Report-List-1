<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Relays market encompasses various types, primarily plug-in relays and PCB relays. Plug-in relays are designed for easy installation and replacement, offering flexibility in configuration for electric vehicles. They are commonly used for battery management and power distribution. PCB relays, on the other hand, are mounted directly onto printed circuit boards, providing compactness and reliability in space-constrained applications. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the efficiency and performance of electric vehicle systems.</p></p>
