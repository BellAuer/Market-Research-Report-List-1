<p><p>The Valves market for cast and ductile iron encompasses various types essential for fluid control in industrial applications. Butterfly valves allow for quick shut-off and are space-efficient. Gate valves are used for on-off services, providing minimal pressure drop. Globe valves offer precise flow regulation and throttling capabilities. Ball valves provide excellent sealing and low-pressure drop in applications, while check valves prevent backflow in systems. Each type serves a specific purpose, reflecting the diverse needs of various industries.</p></p>
