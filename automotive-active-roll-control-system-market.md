<p><ul><li>Electric Active Roll Control (EARC)</li><li>Hydraulic Active Roll Control (HARC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Active Roll Control System market is divided into two main types: Electric Active Roll Control (EARC) and Hydraulic Active Roll Control (HARC). EARC utilizes electric actuators and sensors to adjust suspension parameters in real-time, enhancing stability and comfort during cornering. In contrast, HARC employs hydraulic systems to manage roll dynamics through fluid pressure adjustments, offering precise control of vehicle inclination. Both systems aim to improve vehicle handling, safety, and passenger comfort, catering to evolving automotive performance standards.</p></p>
