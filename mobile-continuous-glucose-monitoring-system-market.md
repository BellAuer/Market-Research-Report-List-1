<p><strong>The Mobile Continuous Glucose Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Invasive</li><li>Non-invasive (Exfoliation)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mobile Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) System market is divided into two primary types: invasive and non-invasive methods. Invasive CGM involves the use of sensors inserted under the skin to measure glucose levels through interstitial fluid, providing real-time data for diabetes management. Non-invasive CGM, including exfoliation techniques, strives to measure glucose levels without skin penetration, often relying on optical or electromagnetic methods. Both types aim to enhance diabetes management but differ in their approach to sensor technology and patient comfort.</p></p>
