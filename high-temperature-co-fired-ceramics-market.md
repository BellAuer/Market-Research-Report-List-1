<p><p>The high-temperature co-fired ceramics (HTCC) market is primarily categorized into two types: Al2O3 HTCC boards and AlN HTCC boards. Al2O3 (alumina) HTCC boards are widely used for their excellent thermal stability and electrical insulation properties, making them suitable for various electronic applications. Conversely, AlN (aluminum nitride) HTCC boards offer superior thermal conductivity, enhancing heat dissipation in electronic devices. Both types are essential in the manufacture of advanced electronic components, serving industries like telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
