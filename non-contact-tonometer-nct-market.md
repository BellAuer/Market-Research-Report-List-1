<p><p>A Non-Contact Tonometer (NCT) is an ophthalmic device used to measure intraocular pressure (IOP) without touching the eye. It employs a puff of air to flatten the cornea, allowing for accurate pressure readings essential for diagnosing conditions like glaucoma. The convenience and increasing preference for non-invasive diagnostic tools drive the expansion of the NCT market.</p><p>The Non-Contact Tonometer (NCT) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.3% during the forecast period. This growth is influenced by advancements in technology, including improved imaging techniques and automated measurement processes, which enhance accuracy and patient comfort. Increasing prevalence of eye disorders, coupled with a growing geriatric population, further propels demand for regular eye examinations and timely diagnosis. </p><p>Additionally, there is a rising emphasis on preventive healthcare, with healthcare providers prioritizing early detection of conditions that can lead to vision impairment.
