<p><p>The seawater desalination membrane market is primarily categorized into three types: Electrodialysis membranes, which use an electric field to drive ions through selective ion-exchange membranes, separating salts from water; Reverse Osmosis membranes, which employ pressure to force seawater through semi-permeable membranes, effectively removing salt and impurities; and Other membranes, encompassing advanced or emerging technologies like forward osmosis and nanofiltration. Each type plays a crucial role in improving water availability and sustainability through desalination processes.</p></p>
