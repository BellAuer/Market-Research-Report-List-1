Gel acrylics offer enhanced workability and reduced shrinkage, ideal for precise layering and detailing in dental applications. Liquid acrylics are primarily used for bonding and repair, providing excellent adhesion properties. Each type serves specific functions in dentistry, catering to diverse needs in dental restoration and prosthetic procedures.</p></p>
