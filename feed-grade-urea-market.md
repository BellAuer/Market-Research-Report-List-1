<p><p>Feed Grade Urea is categorized into two main market types: granule and powder. Granular urea is favored for its slow-release properties, making it ideal for livestock feeds and aiding in controlled nutrient delivery. In contrast, powdered urea offers rapid dissolution, allowing for more immediate absorption by animals but may be less efficient in storage and handling. Both forms are crucial for optimizing livestock nutrition, improving growth rates, and enhancing overall animal health, catering to diverse agricultural needs.</p></p>
