FKM provides superior thermal stability, whereas FSR combines silicone's flexibility with fluoropolymer's chemical resistance. FFKM is used in high-performance seals. Other materials in this market may include specialized formulations for targeted applications across industries like aerospace, automotive, and chemicals.</p></p>
