<p><p>The potassium chloride market is segmented into three main types: powder, crystal, and big granule. Powdered potassium chloride is finely ground, making it suitable for applications requiring quick dissolution, such as fertilizers and pharmaceuticals. Crystal potassium chloride has a larger particle size, commonly used in food processing and as a salt substitute due to its slow dissolution. Big granule potassium chloride features even larger granules, ideal for bulk applications, including agricultural uses, as it promotes controlled release and efficient utilization.</p></p>
