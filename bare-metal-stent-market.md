<p><p>A Bare Metal Stent (BMS) is a small, tube-like device made of metal, used in coronary angioplasty procedures to keep narrowed or blocked arteries open. Unlike drug-eluting stents, BMS do not have a coating that releases medication to prevent restenosis. They are often utilized in cases where the risk of complications from medication release is higher or when patients cannot adhere to prolonged antiplatelet therapy.</p><p>The Bare Metal Stent Market is witnessing steady growth, driven by an increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, rising obesity rates, and an aging population. Technological advancements, such as improved materials and design features of BMS, are also contributing to market expansion. Moreover, cost-effectiveness compared to drug-eluting stents makes BMS appealing in various healthcare settings.</p><p>Latest trends indicate a shift toward minimally invasive procedures and the integration of digital technologies in stent delivery systems. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5% during the forecast period, supported by ongoing innovations and a greater emphasis on patient-centric care in cardiology.
