<p><p>The semiconductor bellows valve market includes two primary types: bellows stop valves and bellows gate valves. Bellows stop valves are designed to provide tight sealing and precise flow control, essential for maintaining cleanliness in semiconductor manufacturing processes. Bellows gate valves, on the other hand, offer minimal resistance to flow and are used to isolate sections of a system. Both types are crucial in applications requiring high purity and reliability, ensuring optimal performance in semiconductor production environments.</p></p>
