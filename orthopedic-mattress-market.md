<p><p>The orthopedic mattress market is divided into two main types: spring and non-spring orthopedic mattresses. Spring orthopedic mattresses utilize a coil system to provide support and promote spinal alignment, offering various firmness levels based on coil configuration. In contrast, non-spring orthopedic mattresses use materials like memory foam, latex, or hybrid constructs to deliver pressure relief and contouring support, adapting to body shape and movement. Both types aim to enhance sleep quality and reduce pain for individuals with orthopedic concerns.</p></p>
