<p><ul><li>Articulated Robots</li><li>Parallel Robots</li><li>SCARA Robots</li><li>Cylindrical Robots</li><li>Cartesian Robots</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial robotic machine market comprises various types, each designed for specific applications. Articulated robots, resembling a human arm, excel in complex tasks with multiple degrees of freedom. Parallel robots feature a fixed base and are known for high speed and precision. SCARA robots are ideal for assembly operations, offering vertical movement and lateral reach. Cylindrical robots provide rotational and linear motion, while Cartesian robots, or gantry robots, utilize a three-axis coordinate system for precise positioning in tasks like material handling.</p></p>
