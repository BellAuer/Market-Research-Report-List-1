<p><p>The mill liner market consists of two main types: metal mill liners and rubber mill liners. Metal mill liners, typically made from steel or alloy materials, offer high durability and resistance to wear, making them suitable for high-impact and high-temperature environments. In contrast, rubber mill liners provide excellent sound absorption and lower noise levels, along with corrosion resistance. Both types cater to specific milling needs in industries such as mining and cement, enhancing operational efficiency and equipment lifespan.</p></p>
