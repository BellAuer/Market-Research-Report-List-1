<p><p>Multibeam echo sounders are classified into three market types based on frequency: low, medium, and high frequency. Low frequency systems are ideal for deep-water surveys, providing wide coverage and penetrating to great depths, making them suitable for offshore mapping. Medium frequency systems balance depth penetration and detail, often used in coastal and continental shelf mapping. High frequency systems focus on high-resolution data collection in shallow waters, delivering detailed images of seabeds, making them ideal for detailed habitat studies and engineering applications.</p></p>
