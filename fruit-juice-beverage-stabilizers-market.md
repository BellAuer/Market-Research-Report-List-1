Gum Arabic acts as an emulsifier, enhancing the flavor profile and clarity of beverages. Carboxymethyl cellulose (CMC) is widely used for its ability to control viscosity. Other stabilizers may include pectin and guar gum, catering to diverse formulation needs in the juice industry.</p></p>
