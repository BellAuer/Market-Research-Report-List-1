<p><p>Renin-inhibitors are a class of antihypertensive medications that work by directly inhibiting the activity of renin, an enzyme crucial in the regulation of blood pressure. The primary drug in this category, Aliskiren, has gained traction in the healthcare market as an alternative treatment for hypertension.</p><p>The Renin-Inhibitors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.8% during the forecast period, driven by an increasing prevalence of hypertension and related cardiovascular diseases. Furthermore, the rising geriatric population, which is more susceptible to hypertension, will contribute to market expansion.</p><p>Recent trends indicate a shift towards combination therapies, where renin-inhibitors are combined with other antihypertensive agents to enhance efficacy and improve patient compliance.
