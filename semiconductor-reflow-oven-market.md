<p><strong>The Semiconductor Reflow Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convection Ovens</li><li>Vapour Phase Ovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor reflow oven market primarily consists of two main types: convection ovens and vapor phase ovens. Convection ovens utilize heated air to transfer energy for soldering components onto printed circuit boards, ensuring even heat distribution. Vapor phase ovens, on the other hand, use vaporized liquids to create a controlled thermal environment, allowing for precise temperature management during the soldering process. Both types are essential for achieving reliable and high-quality solder joints in electronic manufacturing.</p></p>
