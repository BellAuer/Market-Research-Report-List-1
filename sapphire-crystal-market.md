<p><ul><li>Artificial Sapphire Crystal</li><li>Natural Sapphire Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sapphire crystal market is divided into two main types: artificial sapphire crystal and natural sapphire crystal. Artificial sapphire, also known as synthetic sapphire, is manufactured through processes such as the Verneuil or Czochralski methods, offering a cost-effective and consistent alternative for applications like electronics and watchmaking. In contrast, natural sapphire crystals are mined from the earth and prized for their unique colors and rarity, primarily used in luxury jewelry and high-end applications. Both types cater to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
