<p><p>The automotive fuel tank pressure sensor market is segmented into three types based on pressure levels. Medium-pressure sensors, typically ranging from 1 to 5 bars, provide enhanced accuracy for fuel delivery systems. High-pressure sensors, exceeding 5 bars, are crucial for advanced fuel injection systems, facilitating precise measurements for improved engine efficiency and performance while meeting stringent emissions regulations.</p></p>
