<p><p>The commercial ice cream makers market is primarily divided into two types: soft ice cream makers and hard ice cream makers. Soft ice cream makers produce a creamy, smooth consistency ideal for products like soft serve, often incorporating air for a lighter texture. These machines typically offer quick serving and are popular in fast-food establishments. Hard ice cream makers, on the other hand, create denser, traditional ice cream, requiring a freezing process before serving. These are commonly used in ice cream parlors and restaurants for a richer flavor experience.</p></p>
