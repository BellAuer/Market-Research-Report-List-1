<p><p>A recirculating fume cupboard is a ventilation system designed to safeguard laboratory personnel from hazardous fumes, vapors, and particulate matter. Unlike traditional fume hoods, which vent air outside, recirculating fume cupboards filter the air through HEPA or charcoal filters before recirculating it back into the laboratory environment. This system is particularly beneficial in settings where duct installation is impractical or costly, making it a popular choice in various laboratory applications.</p><p>The Recirculating Fume Cupboard Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.9% during the forecast period.
