<p><p>The nanopharmaceuticals market encompasses various types of drug delivery systems. Liposomes are lipid-based carriers enhancing drug solubility and bioavailability. Polymeric micelles improve drug stability and circulation time. Solid lipid nanoparticles offer controlled release and reduce toxicity. Microemulsions facilitate drug absorption through enhanced dispersibility, while nanoemulsions provide improved stability and bioavailability in formulations. Nanosuspensions enhance the solubility and absorption of poorly soluble drugs. Collectively, these technologies enable targeted therapy and improved patient outcomes in various medical applications.</p></p>
