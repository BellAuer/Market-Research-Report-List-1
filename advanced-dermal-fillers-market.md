<p><p>The advanced dermal fillers market is categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable fillers. Absorbable fillers, made from materials like hyaluronic acid, gradually break down in the body, providing temporary volume and support, ideal for enhancing facial features and reducing wrinkles. Non-absorbable fillers, often composed of substances like silicone or PMMA, offer longer-lasting results but require careful administration due to potential complications. Each type serves distinct aesthetic needs, catering to patient preferences and desired outcomes in cosmetic treatments.</p></p>
