<p><p>The marble flooring market can be categorized into three main types based on mineral composition: Dolomite, Magnesium, and Calcite. Dolomite marble, which contains magnesium, is known for its durability and resistance to weathering, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor applications. Magnesium marble features unique color variations and patterns, appealing to aesthetic preferences. Calcite marble, the most common type, is prized for its luster and diverse range of colors, often used in luxury flooring and decorative purposes.</p></p>
