<p><p>Interventional image-guided systems are critical in medical procedures, enhancing accuracy and minimally invasive techniques. Interventional CT image-guided systems utilize computed tomography for precise visualization during interventions. Interventional X-ray systems provide real-time imaging for various procedures, promoting safety and efficiency. MRI image-guided systems employ magnetic resonance imaging for deep tissue guidance, especially in neurosurgery. Interventional ultrasound systems use sound waves for real-time imaging, ideal for soft tissue examinations. Other market types may include emerging technologies like hybrid systems that combine modalities for improved outcomes.</p></p>
