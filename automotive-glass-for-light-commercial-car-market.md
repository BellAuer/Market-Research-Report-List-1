<p><p>The automotive glass market for light commercial vehicles includes several types: tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass is heat-treated for enhanced strength and safety, commonly used in side and rear windows. Laminated glass consists of layers of glass and plastic, providing superior impact resistance and sound attenuation, ideal for windshields. "Others" encompasses specialized glass types like acoustic or tinted glass, which offer additional benefits such as noise reduction or UV protection. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety and comfort.</p></p>
