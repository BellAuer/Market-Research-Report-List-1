<p><p>The solar cell module market comprises several types, each with distinct characteristics. Mono-Si modules, made from single-crystal silicon, offer high efficiency and aesthetics. Multi-Si modules, using silicon wafers from multiple crystals, are cost-effective but slightly less efficient. CdTe modules are thin-film technologies with lower production costs, while CIGS modules combine flexibility and efficiency in a lightweight format. a-Si modules utilize amorphous silicon for a versatile, lightweight option. Other technologies include emerging innovations like organic photovoltaics and BIPV solutions.</p></p>
