<p><p>Compliance Management Systems (CMS) can be categorized into On-Premise and Cloud-Based markets. Conversely, Cloud-Based systems are hosted remotely, providing easy access, scalability, and lower initial costs. They facilitate real-time updates and collaboration, making compliance management more efficient for organizations. Both types serve to streamline compliance processes but cater to different operational needs and preferences.</p></p>
