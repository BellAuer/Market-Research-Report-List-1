<p><p>The Mask Reticle market encompasses various types tailored to specific fabrication needs in the semiconductor industry. Quartz masks, known for their durability and precision, are preferred for high-resolution lithography due to their excellent light transmission and thermal stability. Soda-lime masks, while less expensive, are typically utilized for lower-precision applications. Film masks, made from photosensitive materials, offer flexibility and are suitable for prototyping and low-volume production runs, making them ideal for rapid development processes in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
