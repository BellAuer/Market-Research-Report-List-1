<p><p>The Online Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment market is categorized into two main types: 2D AOI equipment and 3D AOI equipment. </p><p>2D AOI equipment captures images of printed circuit boards (PCBs) from various angles, analyzing defects by comparing them to reference images, making it suitable for basic inspections. </p><p>In contrast, 3D AOI equipment utilizes advanced imaging techniques to provide depth information, enabling more detailed inspections of complex geometries and solder joints. Both types are crucial for ensuring quality in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
