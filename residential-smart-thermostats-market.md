<p><p>The residential smart thermostats market can be categorized into three primary types: WiFi thermostats, ZigBee thermostats, and others. WiFi thermostats connect directly to home internet networks, allowing for remote control via smartphones. ZigBee thermostats use a low-power wireless communication protocol, enabling devices to connect within a smart home ecosystem while consuming less energy. The "Others" category encompasses various proprietary and emerging technologies that provide unique features or interoperability options, catering to diverse consumer preferences and smart home setups.</p></p>
