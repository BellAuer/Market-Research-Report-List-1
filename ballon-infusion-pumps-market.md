<p><p>Balloon infusion pumps are specialized devices used to deliver fluids into a patient's body, primarily for therapeutic purposes. Continuous rate pumps provide a steady, unchanging rate of infusion, ensuring a constant medication delivery over time. In contrast, variable rate pumps allow for adjustable infusion rates, based on patient needs or responses to treatment. Both types cater to different clinical scenarios, enhancing treatment precision and effectiveness while minimizing risks associated with fluid administration. These pumps are essential in critical care and pain management settings.</p></p>
