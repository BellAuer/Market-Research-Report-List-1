<p><p>The automotive force sensor market includes various types, primarily capacitive and piezo-resistive force sensors. Capacitive force sensors detect changes in capacitance due to applied force, offering high sensitivity and precision, ideal for applications like touchscreens and dashboard controls. Piezo-resistive force sensors change resistance when a force is applied, making them suitable for dynamic measurements in tire pressure monitoring and load sensing. Other sensor types may include optical or strain gauge sensors, catering to diverse automotive applications.</p></p>
