<p><ul><li>Separate Chips</li><li>Integrated Chips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile phone chipsets market is categorized into two main types: separate chips and integrated chips. Separate chips consist of individual components, such as the processor, modem, and graphics chip, allowing for greater customization and flexibility in performance. In contrast, integrated chips combine multiple functions into a single chip, offering compact design and energy efficiency. While separate chips provide enhanced capabilities for high-performance devices, integrated chips are favored for their cost-effectiveness and reduced space requirements in smartphones.</p></p>
