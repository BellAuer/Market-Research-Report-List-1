<p><p>A Fingertip Pulse Oximeter is a non-invasive device used to monitor a person's oxygen saturation levels (SpO2) and pulse rate. The device is especially valuable for patients with respiratory conditions, athletes, and anyone needing to monitor their oxygen levels regularly.</p><p>The Fingertip Pulse Oximeter Market is poised for significant growth, driven by an increasing emphasis on home healthcare, rising incidences of respiratory ailments, and the growing geriatric population.
