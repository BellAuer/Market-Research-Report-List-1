<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Handheld Laser Telemeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phase Method Telemeter</li><li>Pulse Method Telemeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld laser telemeter market consists of two primary types: phase method telemeters and pulse method telemeters. Phase method telemeters measure distances by analyzing the phase shift of a laser beam reflected from a target, providing high accuracy and rapid measurements. In contrast, pulse method telemeters calculate distance by emitting short laser pulses and measuring the time it takes for the pulse to return. Both types are widely used in construction, surveying, and various industrial applications for precise distance measurements.</p></p>
