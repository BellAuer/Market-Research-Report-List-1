<p><p>The Dual Power Transfer Switches market is divided into two primary types: automatic and manual. Automatic transfer switches (ATS) enable seamless switching between power sources without human intervention, ensuring uninterrupted power supply during outages or disturbances. In contrast, manual transfer switches require operators to manually switch sources, offering greater control but necessitating a physical presence and potentially causing delays during power failures. Both types are essential for enhancing reliability in electrical systems across various applications, including commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
