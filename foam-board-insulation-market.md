<p><ul><li>EPS Foam Board</li><li>XPS Foam Board</li><li>Polyurethane Foam Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Foam Board Insulation market is primarily segmented into three types: EPS (Expanded Polystyrene), XPS (Extruded Polystyrene), and Polyurethane foam boards. EPS is lightweight and cost-effective, providing moderate insulation and moisture resistance, ideal for various applications. XPS offers superior thermal insulation and moisture resistance, making it suitable for below-grade and exterior applications. Polyurethane foam boards deliver high insulation values in thinner profiles, often used in specialized building applications. Each type caters to specific insulation needs and performance requirements in construction and renovation projects.</p></p>
