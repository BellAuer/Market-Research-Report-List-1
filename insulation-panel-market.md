<p><p>The insulation panel market comprises various types, including Vacuum Insulated Panels (VIPs), Structural Insulated Panels (SIPs), and others. VIPs provide high thermal resistance with minimal thickness, making them ideal for space-constrained applications. SIPs consist of a rigid foam core sandwiched between two structural facings, offering both insulation and structural strength, suitable for walls, roofs, and floors. Other types may include mineral wool panels and fiberglass panels, catering to diverse insulation needs across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
