<p><p>The Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) treatment market encompasses several approaches: Medicine involves the use of pharmaceuticals to manage symptoms and slow disease progression. Hemodialysis is a process where a machine filters waste from the blood, typically done in a clinic or at home. Peritoneal dialysis utilizes the lining of the abdominal cavity to cleanse the blood, offering greater flexibility for patients. Kidney transplantation involves surgically replacing a failed kidney with a healthy one, providing a potential cure for advanced CKD.</p></p>
