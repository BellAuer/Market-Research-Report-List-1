Dimer acid modification utilizes dimer acids to enhance flexibility and chemical resistance. Urethane modification introduces urethane groups for improved elasticity and durability. Additionally, other modifications may include various polymer blends and additives tailored to specific applications, ensuring broad usability across industries like automotive, construction, and aerospace.</p></p>
