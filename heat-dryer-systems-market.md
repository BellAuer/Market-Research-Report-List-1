<p><p>Heat dryer systems are essential in various industries for moisture removal from materials. The market is primarily divided into two types: Direct Type and Indirect Type. Direct Type systems utilize direct contact between the heating medium and the material, allowing for quick drying but often resulting in potential contamination. In contrast, Indirect Type systems involve heat transfer without direct contact, minimizing contamination risk but typically requiring longer drying times. Both types cater to specific applications based on efficiency, quality, and operational needs.</p></p>
