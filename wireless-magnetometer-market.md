<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Wireless Magnetometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scalar Magnetometers</li><li>Vector Magnetometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless magnetometer market is categorized into two main types: scalar magnetometers and vector magnetometers. Scalar magnetometers measure the magnitude of an ambient magnetic field without direction, making them ideal for simple applications such as geological surveys. In contrast, vector magnetometers capture both the magnitude and direction of the magnetic field, providing detailed information useful for applications in navigation, mineral exploration, and defense. The choice between these types depends on the specific requirements of the measurement task.</p></p>
