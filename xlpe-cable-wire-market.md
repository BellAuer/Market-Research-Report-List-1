<p><p>XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) cables and wires are categorized based on their voltage ratings into Low Voltage (LV), Medium Voltage (MV), High Voltage (HV), and Extra High Voltage (EHV) cables. LV cables are used for electrical distribution in residential and commercial applications. MV cables facilitate urban and industrial power distribution. HV cables transmit electricity over long distances with reduced losses, while EHV cables are designed for ultra-high voltage transmission, catering to extensive grid networks and major power supply infrastructures.</p></p>
