<p><ul><li>Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H)</li><li>Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polybutylene Oxygen Barrier Pipes market consists of two main types: Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H) and Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R). PB-H features a linear structure, offering high rigidity and excellent resistance to stress and impact, making it suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, PB-R delivers enhanced flexibility and improved impact resistance due to its random copolymer structure, making it ideal for applications requiring bendability and adaptability. Both types provide effective oxygen barrier properties, crucial for plumbing and heating systems.</p></p>
