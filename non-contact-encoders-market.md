<p><p>Non-contact encoders utilize various technologies to measure position without physical contact, ensuring durability and precision. Magnetic encoders employ magnetic fields to detect movement, making them robust in harsh environments. Capacitive encoders use changes in capacitance to determine position, offering high resolution in compact designs. Optical encoders rely on light transmission through notched discs or strips, providing very accurate position feedback. Each type serves specific applications across industries, balancing factors like accuracy, environmental resistance, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
