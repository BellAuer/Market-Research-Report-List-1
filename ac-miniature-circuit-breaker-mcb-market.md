<p><p>The AC Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB) market is categorized into several types based on their response characteristics to overcurrent conditions. Type B MCBs trip between 3 to 5 times the rated current, ideal for residential circuits with moderate loads. Type C MCBs trip between 5 to 10 times the rated current, suitable for commercial applications with high inrush currents. Type D MCBs trip between 10 to 20 times the rated current, designed for heavy industrial equipment. 'Others' may include specialized breakers for unique environments or applications.</p></p>
