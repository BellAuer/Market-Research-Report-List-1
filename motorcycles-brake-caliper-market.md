<p><p>The motorcycle brake caliper market is primarily categorized into single piston and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers utilize one piston to engage the brake pads, offering simplicity and lighter weight, making them popular in smaller bikes and low-speed applications. In contrast, multi-piston calipers feature multiple pistons to provide more braking force and improved heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-performance motorcycles and racing applications. Each type serves distinct motorcycle segments, balancing performance, weight, and cost considerations.</p></p>
