<p><strong>The Immunochromatographic Assay Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sandwich Assay</li><li>Competitive Assay</li><li>Multiplex Detection Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunochromatographic assay kit market comprises several types of assays, including sandwich assays, competitive assays, and multiplex detection assays. Sandwich assays involve the immobilization of an antigen and binding with labeled antibodies, offering high specificity and sensitivity. Competitive assays, on the other hand, utilize a competitive mechanism where the sample antigen competes with a labeled antigen for binding sites, suitable for small analytes. Multiplex detection assays enable simultaneous detection of multiple targets in a single test, enhancing efficiency and throughput.</p></p>
