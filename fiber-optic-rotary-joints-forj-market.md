The market is segmented into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel FORJs transmit data over one optical path, ideal for applications with limited data needs. In contrast, multi-channel FORJs facilitate multiple data paths, supporting high-bandwidth applications and allowing simultaneous transmission of various signals, thereby enhancing operational efficiency in complex systems.</p></p>
