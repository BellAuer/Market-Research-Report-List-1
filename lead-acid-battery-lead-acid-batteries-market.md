<p><p>Lead-acid batteries are widely used for energy storage due to their reliability and cost-effectiveness. The market primarily includes two types: VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid) batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed, maintenance-free, and exhibit better performance in varied orientations, making them ideal for applications like UPS systems and renewable energy. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, require regular maintenance, offer higher capacity, and are commonly used in automotive and large-scale energy storage systems.</p></p>
