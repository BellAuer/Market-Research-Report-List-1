<p><strong>The Optical Semiconductor Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dark Field Inspection</li><li>Bright Field Inspection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical semiconductor inspection equipment primarily includes dark field and bright field inspection techniques. Dark field inspection enhances contrast by illuminating the sample at an angle, allowing the detection of surface defects and contamination without interference from reflected light. In contrast, bright field inspection uses direct illumination, providing a clear view of the semiconductor’s structures and defects illuminated from behind. Both methods are essential for ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor manufacturing, addressing distinct inspection needs.</p></p>
