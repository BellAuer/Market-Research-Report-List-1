<p><p>The instrument tubes market is categorized into seamless and welded types. Seamless tubes are manufactured without any joints, offering superior strength and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Welded tubes, on the other hand, are created by fusing metal sheets or strips, resulting in a more cost-effective solution suitable for lower pressure applications. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and chemical processing, ensuring reliable performance in instrumentation systems.</p></p>
