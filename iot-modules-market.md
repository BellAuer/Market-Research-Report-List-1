<p><strong>The IoT Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LPWANs</li><li>Cellular (3G/4G/5G)</li><li>Zigbee</li><li>Bluetooth and BLE</li><li>Wi-Fi</li><li>RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT modules market includes various connectivity types tailored for different use cases. LPWANs are ideal for long-range, low-power applications. Cellular networks (3G/4G/5G) provide wide coverage and high data rates, suitable for mobile IoT. Zigbee is effective for short-range, low-power mesh networks, often used in smart homes. Bluetooth and BLE enable short-range connections with minimal energy consumption. Wi-Fi offers high-speed internet access, while RFID facilitates contactless data transmission for tracking and identification purposes. Each technology serves specific needs in the IoT ecosystem.</p></p>
