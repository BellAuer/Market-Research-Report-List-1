<p><p>The desktop hypervisor market can be divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. On-premises hypervisors, on the other hand, are installed directly on local hardware, providing greater control, security, and performance for organizations that require dedicated resources. Both types cater to varying business needs, balancing user accessibility with deployment preferences.</p></p>
