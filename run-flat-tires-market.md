<p><p>The run-flat tire market is primarily divided into two types: self-supporting and support ring systems. Self-supporting tires are designed with reinforced sidewalls that allow them to maintain their shape and support vehicle weight even when deflated. In contrast, support ring systems utilize an additional supportive structure within the tire that holds the tire shape and allows continued driving after a loss of pressure. Both types enhance safety and convenience, enabling drivers to continue their journey without immediate tire replacement.</p></p>
