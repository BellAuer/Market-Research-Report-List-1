<p><p>The dental orthodontic adhesives market comprises various types, including chemically-cured, light-cured, dual-cured, and other adhesives. Chemically-cured adhesives set through a chemical reaction, providing a strong bond without the need for light. Light-cured adhesives utilize ultraviolet or visible light for rapid curing, allowing for precise application. Dual-cured adhesives combine both methods, offering flexibility in application and ensuring reliable bonding. Other types may include specialty adhesives tailored for specific dental procedures, enhancing versatility in orthodontic treatments.</p></p>
