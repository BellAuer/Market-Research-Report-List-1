<p><p>The Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) system market comprises several types, including the Turbulent Jet (T) system, Piezo Fuel Injector, and Water Jet system. The Turbulent Jet system enhances fuel atomization for improved combustion efficiency. Piezo Fuel Injectors utilize piezoelectric elements for precise fuel delivery, providing faster response times and better control over injection timing. The Water Jet system incorporates water injection to minimize knock and enhance performance, promoting cleaner emissions and greater fuel efficiency. Each type contributes to advanced engine performance and efficiency.</p></p>
